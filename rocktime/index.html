Artists

Jim Morrison

American singer, songwriter and poet who was the lead vocalist and primary lyricist of the rock band the Doors.Morrison is regarded by music critics and fans as one of the most influential frontmen in rock history.

Freddie Mercury

British singer and songwriter who achieved worldwide fame as the lead vocalist and pianist of the rock band Queen. Regarded as one of the greatest singers in the history of rock music, he was known for his flamboyant stage persona and four-octave vocal range.

Ray Charles

American singer, songwriter and pianist. He is regarded as one of the most iconic and influential musicians in history, and was often referred to by contemporaries as "The Genius". Among friends and fellow musicians he preferred being called "Brother Ray".

John Lennon

English singer, songwriter and musician. He gained worldwide fame as the founder, co-lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist of the Beatles. His work included music, writing, drawings and film.
